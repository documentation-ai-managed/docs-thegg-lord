## Overview

To organize your projects, create separate documentation spaces in Thegg Lord.

## Steps

1. Log in to your Thegg Lord account
2. Click the "+ New Space" button on the dashboard
3. Enter a name for your documentation space
4. Click "Create"

Your new space appears on your dashboard. Start adding content right away!
