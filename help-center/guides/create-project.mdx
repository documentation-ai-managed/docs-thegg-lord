## Quick Start

Follow these steps to start organizing your documentation projects in Thegg Lord.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    From your dashboard, click on "Projects" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new project">
    Click the "+ New Project" button and fill in your project details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start documenting">
    Select your new project and click "Add Document" to begin writing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
