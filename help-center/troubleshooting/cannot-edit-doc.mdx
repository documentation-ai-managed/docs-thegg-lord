description: How to resolve editing access issues in Thegg Lord.
---

## Symptoms

- Edit button is disabled
- Changes are not saved

## Causes

- Insufficient permissions
- Not signed in

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check sign-in status">
    Make sure you are logged into Thegg Lord.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify permissions">
    Ask the space owner to grant you editing rights.
  </Step>
</Steps>
